# Product Mission (Lite)

ZenSkin is a D2C wellness brand helping health-conscious consumers achieve radiant skin through gut health optimization with scientifically-backed supplements and personalized routines.

ZenSkin targets urban Indian millennials (25-40) who understand the gut-skin connection and seek natural, lasting solutions over quick fixes. Unlike traditional skincare brands, we address root causes through gut microbiome optimization, backed by clinical research, delivered through an integrated digital experience across web and mobile platforms.
